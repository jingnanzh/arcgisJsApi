Build an app to interactively select and display an ArcGIS Online basemap:

      To build an application that can switch between basemaps, you can use the Basemap Toggle or the Basemap Gallery widget.
      The Basemap Gallery widget allows you to select from a number of basemaps that belong to an ArcGIS Online group.

      You can use a group of basemaps hosted by Esri or a group that you have composed specifically for your application.
      To learn more about designing and styling your own basemaps, visit the Style a vector basemap tutorial.

      https://developers.arcgis.com/javascript/latest/guide/select-a-basemap/
